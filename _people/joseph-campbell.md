Joseph leads the CAMP Lab, where the goal is to build agents capable of lifelong learning.
A key piece of this is the ability to *introspect*, to understand their own knowledge and capabilities.
He explores this through interpretability, sample-efficient learning, theory of mind, and human–robot interaction.
